# 🧬 Clinical Trial Enrollment AI Dashboard

An end-to-end machine learning + NLP + LLM-style decision intelligence platform designed to predict clinical trial enrollment risk and enable proactive intervention.

## 🔗 Live Demo
(https://trialenrollment.streamlit.app/)

---

## 🚀 Problem
Clinical trials often miss enrollment targets due to:
- Late identification of underperforming sites
- Lack of actionable insights for intervention
- Fragmented operational data

---

## 💡 Solution
This dashboard provides early signal detection and decision support using:

- 📈 **Enrollment Forecasting** (Random Forest Regression)
- ⚠️ **Site Risk Modeling** (AUC-based classification)
- 🧠 **NLP Barrier Analysis** (site-level issue detection)
- 🤖 **LLM-style Executive Summaries** for leadership decision-making

---

## 🧠 Key Features

- Predicts whether trials will meet enrollment targets
- Identifies high-risk and zero-enrollment sites
- Segments performance by region
- Extracts common operational barriers (NLP)
- Generates human-readable insights (LLM-style)

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- Python
- Streamlit
- Scikit-learn
- Pandas
- Plotly

---

## 📊 Business Impact

- Enables early intervention in underperforming trials
- Improves site selection and allocation strategy
- Reduces risk of missed enrollment targets
- Bridges gap between data science and clinical operations
